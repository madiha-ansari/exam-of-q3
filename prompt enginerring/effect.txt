Effect of Temperature

Low Temperature (0–0.3):
→ Output hamesha safe, predictable, aur factual hoga.
→ Example: “Pakistan ka capital?” → Islamabad (har baar wahi jawab).

High Temperature (0.7–1.0+):
→ Output zyada creative, diverse aur kabhi-kabhi irrelevant bhi ho sakta hai.
→ Example: Har baar wording change hogi, kabhi poetry touch bhi aa sakta hai.

2. Effect of Top-k

Chhota k (e.g. k=5):
→ Sirf 5 sabse probable words me se select karega.
→ Effect: Output focused aur less creative.

Bada k (e.g. k=100):
→ Zyada options me se choose karega.
→ Effect: Output diverse aur creative.

3. Effect of Top-p

Chhota p (e.g. p=0.2):
→ Sirf 20% probability cover hone tak words lega.
→ Effect: Safe aur predictable answers.

Bada p (e.g. p=0.9):
→ Zyada wide range ke words consider karega.
→ Effect: Zyada variety aur creativity.

4. Overall Effect Together

Agar Temperature low + Top-k/p low → Output bohot safe aur har baar same type ka hoga.

Agar Temperature high + Top-k/p high → Output zyada creative, kabhi-kabhi ghalat bhi ho sakta hai.

Balance rakho to output useful aur thoda creative dono ho sakta hai.

👉 Simple words me:

Low values → Safe & consistent answers

High values → Creative & random answers
